Chartwell

SEI
SEI is a boutique business and technology consulting firm headquartered in Cincinnati and founded in 1992. The firm employs more than 600 experienced consultants across 13 U.S. offices and operates under an employee-owned model.

SEI partners with organizations on strategy and operations, technology enablement, data and analytics, security, and enterprise transformation initiatives. Its delivery approach emphasizes seasoned practitioners and local staffing, with consultants living and working near client sites to reduce travel and support sustained engagement.

By hiring exclusively experienced professionals and avoiding traditional entry-level recruiting, SEI maintains a senior-heavy delivery model focused on practical execution and long-term client relationships.

Cirque Analytics
Cirque Analytics is a boutique economic consulting firm headquartered in Jackson Hole, Wyoming, with four U.S. offices. The firm provides expert testimony and advisory services on complex economic, financial, and statistical matters, including antitrust, class actions, commercial disputes, and advanced data analysis.

Cirque focuses on technically rigorous litigation support, combining quantitative modeling with close collaboration alongside clients and legal teams. Its engagements frequently involve high-profile disputes requiring detailed empirical analysis and expert reporting.

With approximately 50 employees, Cirque maintains a mentorship-oriented environment in which consultants assume meaningful responsibility early. The firm operates with a low-travel model, supporting in-person collaboration while minimizing traditional consulting travel demands.
